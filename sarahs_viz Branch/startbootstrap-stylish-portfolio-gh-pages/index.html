Search Tips & Tricks
Like a fine wine, our custom database requires finesse and precision. Follow these tips to ensure a great result:

  • For Price, entire whole dollar amounts.
  • This database has U.S. wines, and they're all pretty good. Stick with points scores from 80 to 100.
  • We'll save you some time: The cheapest price you'll find is $4.
  • IMPORTANT: All fields must be filled in for the search to work!
  • If you don't get a result, change one or more of your values and try again.
  • The states included are: California, Oregon, Washington, New York, Virginia, Idaho, Texas, Michigan, New Mexico, Arizona, Missouri, Pennsylvania, Ohio, North Carolina, New Jersey, Colorado, Massachusetts, Illinois, Iowa, Connecticut, Vermont, Kentucky, Nevada, Hawaii, and Rhode Island. (Rhode Island has one entry, a Champagne Blend with a point score of 90 that costs $25.)
